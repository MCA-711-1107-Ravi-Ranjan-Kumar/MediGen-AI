How MediGen-AI Works

Seamless end-to-end flow — from hospital registration to doorstep medicine delivery.

1

🏥 Patient Arrives at Hospital

OPD Staff registers the patient — name, age, weight, mobile, ABHA Address. A unique Patient UID is auto-generated and patient joins the smart digital queue.

2

🩺 Doctor Reviews & Prescribes

Doctor logs in, sees waiting list, selects patient. Clicks AI Suggest — AI instantly generates a prescription draft. Doctor reviews, edits, and finalizes with digital signature.

3

📄 Patient Downloads Prescription

Patient logs in with email. The finalized prescription appears immediately. They can Print / PDF for records or pharmacy use.

4

⚡ Instant Medicine Delivery via Blinkit

Every medicine has a direct 1mg order link. One click — medicines ordered from nearest pharmacy. Delivered in 10 minutes.

Key Features

Everything needed for a complete, intelligent digital healthcare experience.

AI Symptom Checker

NLP-powered symptom analysis using Google Gemini / OpenAI to identify conditions and suggest treatment.

Smart Prescription

Auto-generated digital prescriptions with medicines, dosages, and follow-up advice — fully editable by doctor.

Digital Signature

Every prescription verified by a licensed doctor before delivery — Human-in-the-Loop safety.

PDF / Print

Professionally formatted prescription printable by patients for records or pharmacy use.

Medician portal Integration

One-click Quick commerce medicine ordering per prescription line — 10-minute doorstep delivery.

Role-Based Auth

4 roles (Admin/Doctor/Staff/Patient) with SHA-256 encrypted passwords and Flask session management.
